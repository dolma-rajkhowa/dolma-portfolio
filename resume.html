Profile

Former Economics undergrad at Sophia College, Mumbai—pretty chill about graphs, less chill about layouts. I started designing posters for Kaleidoscope Fest and fell harder than expected, eventually dropping out to focus on design. Since then I’ve worked across social media, short-form edits, event visuals, and most recently begun motion design and creative coding to expand what I can build.

During that process, I realized I can spot what good looks like. The harder part is building it myself. That’s the gap I’m working to close—through smarter tools, sharper decisions, and doing the work. My goal is a creative system where design, motion, and code work together, not in silos.
